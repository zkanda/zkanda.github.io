First Time Street Photography: PhotoWalk in Cebu

This was a first time experience and somehow it is easier said than done. Getting creative shots on the street is not an easy task.

Me and a friend decided to met up in a coffee shop first where they have this unique interior.

The meeting place for the Photowalk is at Magellan’s Cross at 11am, it was quite late, so if you’re in the place where it’s hot and humid, pack lightly and use breathable clothes.

One thing that stood out was how intense the light from the sun. It was so bright that taking a shot with shadows is hard, so what I did was to underexposed the shot because it’s easier to recover dark image in post process using Adobe Lightroom but still very limited to what I can do.
